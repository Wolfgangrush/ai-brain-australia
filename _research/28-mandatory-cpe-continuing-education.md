## Authority Tier
Bar / Regulator Rule

## Currency Warning
Stable annual requirement. Note the new "Equality and Wellbeing" mandatory unit for barristers starting 2026.

## Key Facts
1. **10 Units per Year:** Practitioners must complete 10 CPD units between 1 April and 31 March.
2. **The 4 Mandatory Categories:**
    - Ethics and Professional Responsibility.
    - Practice Management and Business Skills.
    - Professional Skills.
    - Substantive Law.
3. **Self-Assessment:** Practitioners must maintain their own records; regulators perform random audits.
4. **Format:** Can be face-to-face, online (webinars/videos), or private study (limited).
5. **Penalty:** Failure to comply can result in a "restricted" practicing certificate or a fine.

## Verbatim Quote (if applicable)
"A practitioner must complete at least one CPD unit in each of the following mandatory areas... Ethics and Professional Responsibility; Practice Management and Business Skills; Professional Skills; Substantive Law." — Rule 6.1.

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
- Solos must be diligent in tracking their own units as they don't have a firm's "CPD Coordinator."
- Solos often struggle to find time for face-to-face seminars; on-demand digital CPD is the most practical solution.
- The "Practice Management" unit is highly relevant for solos to avoid common regulatory pitfalls (trust accounting errors).
