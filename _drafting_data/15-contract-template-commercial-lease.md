## Authority Tier

## Currency Warning
Practitioners should use the **2021 Edition** of the Law Society Lease. Check if the lease is a **"Retail Lease"**; if so, the *Retail Leases Act 1994* (NSW) mandates additional disclosures (Lessor's Disclosure Statement) and restricts certain outgoings (e.g., land tax recovery).

## Template Type
Contract

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Annexure A (Reference Schedule):**
   - Parties (Lessor/Lessee).
   - Description of Premises and Title reference.
   - Term and Options to Renew.
   - Commencement and Expiry Dates.
   - Base Rent and Rent Review (Fixed, CPI, or Market).
   - Outgoings (Percentage and types).
   - Permitted Use.
   - Security (Bank Guarantee or Cash Bond).
2. **Annexure B (Standard Provisions):**
   - Payment of rent and outgoings.
   - Maintenance and repair obligations.
   - Insurance requirements (Public Liability, Glass, Plate).
   - Assignment and sub-letting.
   - Alterations and "Make Good" obligations at expiry.
   - Default, re-entry, and termination.
   - Dispute resolution.
3. **Execution Clause:** Including execution by individuals and companies (s 127).

## Drafting Conventions
- **Registration:** If the term (including options) exceeds 3 years, the lease must be in "registrable form" and registered at NSW Land Registry Services (LRS).
- **Outgoings:** Be specific about whether Land Tax is recoverable (prohibited for retail leases in NSW).
- **Incentives:** If a rent-free period or fit-out contribution is provided, document it in a separate **Incentive Deed** to keep the face of the lease "clean" for valuation purposes.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
> "The Lessee must keep the Premises clean and tidy and in good repair and condition, having regard to their condition at the commencement of this Lease, fair wear and tear excepted." (Standard maintenance clause).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The standard for small commercial property transactions in NSW. Used for shops, offices, and industrial units. Crucial for managing the long-term relationship between landlord and tenant.
