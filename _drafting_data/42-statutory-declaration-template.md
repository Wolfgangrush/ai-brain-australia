## Authority Tier
Statutory Form (Commonwealth)

## Currency Warning
The *Statutory Declarations Amendment Act 2023* made digital statutory declarations and remote witnessing permanent. Ensure the "Digital Identity" option via myGov is considered for clients who cannot access a physical witness.

## Template Type
Statutory Declaration

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Header:** "Commonwealth of Australia - STATUTORY DECLARATION".
2. **Declarant Details:** Name, Address, Occupation.
3. **The Declaration:** "I... make the following declaration under the Statutory Declarations Act 1959..."
4. **Body:** Numbered facts.
5. **The Warning Clause:** "I understand that a person who intentionally makes a false statement in a statutory declaration is guilty of an offence under section 11 of the Statutory Declarations Act 1959, and I believe that the statements in this declaration are true in every particular."
6. **Execution Block:**
   - Signature of Declarant.
   - Place and Date.
   - Signature and details of Witness (from the list of authorized persons).

## Drafting Conventions
- **Approved Form:** Must use the form prescribed by the *Statutory Declarations Regulations 2023*.
- **No Hearsay:** While less strict than affidavits, declarations should focus on facts known to the declarant.
- **Witness Eligibility:** The witness must be on the "prescribed list" (e.g., Lawyer, GP, Pharmacist, Police Officer).

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
> "I understand that a person who intentionally makes a false statement in a statutory declaration is guilty of an offence under section 11 of the Statutory Declarations Act 1959, and I believe that the statements in this declaration are true in every particular." (Mandatory statutory wording).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used for non-court evidence (e.g., insurance claims, government applications, visa support). Essential for administrative law practice and general commercial advisory.
