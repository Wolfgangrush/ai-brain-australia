## Currency Warning
For loans between private companies and shareholders, ensure the agreement complies with **Division 7A** of the *Income Tax Assessment Act 1936* (Cth). Failure to use the mandatory interest rate and term (usually 7 years unsecured) results in the loan being treated as a taxable dividend.

## Template Type
Contract

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties:** Lender and Borrower (including ACN/ABN).
2. **Loan Amount (The Principal):** The total sum to be advanced.
3. **Conditions Precedent:** Requirements before drawdown (e.g., executing security, providing ILA certificate).
4. **Interest:**
   - Interest rate (Fixed or Variable).
   - Default interest rate (must not be a "penalty" under Australian law).
5. **Repayment:**
   - Repayment schedule (Interest-only, Principal + Interest, or Bullet).
   - Early repayment provisions.
6. **Security:**
   - Reference to Mortgage or General Security Agreement (GSA).
   - Right to register on the PPSR.
7. **Events of Default:**
   - Non-payment.
   - Insolvency.
   - Breach of other covenants.
8. **Representations and Warranties:** Borrower's legal capacity and financial state.
9. **Costs and Expenses:** Borrower to pay Lender's legal costs of drafting.
10. **Governing Law:** New South Wales.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Penalty Interest:** To be enforceable, the "default rate" is usually drafted as a "higher rate" that is discounted if the borrower pays on time (the "lower rate").
- **Independent Legal Advice (ILA):** Crucial for lenders to avoid claims of "unconscionable conduct" or "undue influence" (*Amadio* principle).
- **Execution:** Companies must execute under s 127 of the *Corporations Act*.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
> "The Borrower must pay interest on the Principal at the Interest Rate. If the Borrower fails to pay any amount on the due date, the Borrower must pay interest on that amount at the Default Interest Rate from the due date until the date of payment."

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used for intra-family loans, private commercial lending, or "Division 7A" compliance for small business owners. Ensures the lender has a clear path to recovery and valid security on the PPSR.
